Sure! Here is the recipe to make the grilled chicken wrap for three people: 

Ingredients: 
- 3 large whole wheat tortillas 
- 3 tablespoons of olive oil 
- 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 
- 3/4 teaspoon of garlic powder 
- 3/4 teaspoon of Italian seasoning 
- 1/2 teaspoon of paprika 
- Salt and pepper, to taste 
- 3 tablespoons of hummus 
- 3 cups of lettuce 
- 3/4 cup of chopped tomatoes 
- 3/4 cup of chopped red onion 
- 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese 

Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F. 

2. In a large bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, paprika, and salt and pepper. 

3. Add the chicken breasts and toss to coat. 

4. Place the chicken on a baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through. 

5. Let the chicken cool for a few minutes. Once it is cool enough to handle, shred the chicken with two forks. 

6. Spread the hummus on a tortilla, top with lettuce, tomatoes, red onion, chicken, and feta cheese. 

7. Roll the tortilla and enjoy.
