- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 cup of all-purpose flour
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup of breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 zucchini
- 2 bell peppers

Instructions:

1. Cut the chicken breasts into small strips or cubes.

2. Place the flour in a shallow bowl and season with salt and pepper.

3. Dip the chicken into the flour and coat evenly.

4. Slice the zucchini and bell peppers into thin strips.

5. Heat the oil (Vegetable or olive) in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

6. Place the eggs in a shallow bowl and beat until combined.

7. Place the veggie strips in a large bowl and season with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat.

8. Place the zucchini and bell peppers in the air fryer basket in a single layer.

9. Cook for 10 minutes at 375 F, flipping halfway through.

10. Dip the chicken pieces into the egg, then into the breadcrumbs, making sure they are completely coated.

11. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken pieces and cook for 4-5 minutes, until golden brown and cooked through.
