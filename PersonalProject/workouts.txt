Day 1:

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of brisk walking or marching in place.
Stretching: Perform the stretches you listed: Pendulum, Crossover Arm Stretch, Passive Internal Rotation, Passive External Rotation, Sleeper Stretch.
Exercises: Standing Row, External Rotation with Arm Abducted 90 degrees, Internal Rotation, External Rotation, Elbow Flexion, Elbow Extension.
Additional exercises:
Lateral Raises: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a towel in each hand. Lift your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the floor, then lower back down. Repeat for 10-12 reps.
Reverse Flyes: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a towel in each hand. Bend forward at the waist, keeping your back straight. Extend your arms straight out to the sides, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Slowly lower the arms back down. Repeat for 10-12 reps.
Cool-down: 5 minutes of gentle walking and full-body stretches.
Day 2:

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of low-impact cardio exercises like marching or cycling in place.
Stretching: Perform the stretches you listed: Trapezius Strengthening, Scapula Setting, Scapular Retraction/Protraction, Bent-Over Horizontal Abduction, Internal and External Rotation.
Exercises: Head Rolls, Kneeling Back Extensions, Sitting Rotation Stretch.
Additional exercises:
Superman Exercise: Lie on your stomach with your arms extended overhead. Lift your chest, arms, and legs off the ground simultaneously, hold for a few seconds, then lower back down. Repeat for 10-12 reps.
Glute Bridge: Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Lift your hips off the ground until your body forms a straight line from your shoulders to your knees. Hold for a few seconds, then lower back down. Repeat for 10-12 reps.
Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching, focusing on the neck, back, and shoulders.
Day 3:

Rest day or engage in light physical activity like walking, swimming, or yoga to promote recovery.
Day 4:

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light aerobic activity like marching or spot jogging.
Stretching: Perform the stretches you listed: Modified Seat Side Straddle, Knee to Chest, Bird Dog.
Exercises: Plank, Modified Side Plank, Hip Bridge.
Additional exercises:
Wall Sit: Stand with your back against a wall and slide down until your thighs are parallel to the floor. Hold this position for 30-60 seconds.
Clamshell: Lie on your side with knees bent and feet together. Keeping your feet touching, lift your top knee as high as possible without rotating your hips. Lower back down. Repeat for 10-12 reps on each side.
Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching for the entire body.
Day 5:

Rest day or light activity such as walking, stretching, or gentle yoga.
Day 6:

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of gentle cardio exercises like marching or cycling.
Stretching: Perform the stretches you listed: Abdominal Bracing, Abdominal Crunch.
Exercises: Repeat the exercises from Day 1 and Day 2.
Additional exercises:
Side Plank: Lie on your side, propping yourself up on your forearm. Lift your hips off the ground, creating a straight line from your head to your feet. Hold for 30-60 seconds
