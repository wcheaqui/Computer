Workout Routine for PickleBall - Lower Body Emphasis
Warm-up (5-10 minutes):
Activation (5 minutes):
	•	Glute bridges: 2 sets of 15 reps
	•	Leg swings: 2 sets of 10 swings each leg
Dynamic Stretching (5-10 minutes):
Goblet Squats:
	•	Sets: 3, Reps: 10-12, Rest: 60-90 seconds
Romanian Deadlifts:
	•	Sets: 3, Reps: 8-10, Rest: 60-90 seconds
Bulgarian Split Squats (using dumbbells or barbell):
	•	Sets: 3 each leg, Reps: 10-12, Rest: 60-90 seconds
Leg Press:
	•	Sets: 3, Reps: 12-15, Rest: 60-90 seconds
Calf Raises (using machine or free weights):
	•	Sets: 3, Reps: 15-20, Rest: 45-60 seconds

Pickleball Practice Routine:
    Against Wall
        Forehand and Backhand Dink Practice:
            Repetitions: 50 dinks each side
        Forehand and Backhand Dink Past Body:
            Repetitions: 50 dinks each side
        Volleys:
            Repetitions: 50 volleys each side
        Double Tap Volleys:
            Repetitions: 25 each side
        Volleys 1, 2, 3 with Resets:
            Repetitions: 20 - 1s, 15 - 2s, 10 - 3s: each side
        Forehand and Backhand Return Practice (From 22 Feet Away):
            Repetitions: 50 returns each side
        Drop Shot Practice:
            Repetitions: 25 drop shots each side
    Serving Practice:
        Repetitions: 50 serves each side
