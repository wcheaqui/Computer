Warm-up (5-10 minutes):
	•	Light cardio to increase blood flow and warm up your muscles.
	•	Dynamic stretches to improve flexibility and range of motion.
Strength Training:
		Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
	•	Focus on your form and depth to engage your leg muscles effectively.
		Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
	•	Use proper technique and gradually increase the weight as you progress.
		Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
	•	Work on chest and triceps strength to improve your hitting power.
		Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
	•	Strengthen your upper back and shoulders for better control and precision.
		Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
	•	Enhance leg strength and stability for quick movements on the court.
		Core Exercises:
	•	Planks: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds each.
	•	Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps (use a weighted medicine ball if available).
		Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
	•	Strengthen your shoulders for improved serving and overhead shots.
		Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
	•	Enhance tricep strength, essential for a powerful follow-through in your shots.
Cool Down (5-10 minutes):
	•	Static stretches for major muscle groups to improve flexibility and aid recovery.
	•	Deep breathing or light yoga poses to help relax your body.
