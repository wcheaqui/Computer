Sure! Here is the recipe for the chicken salad: 

Ingredients: 
- 2 cups of shredded cooked chicken 
- 1/2 cup of mayonnaise 
- 1/2 cup of diced celery 
- 1/2 cup of diced red onion 
- 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley 
- 1 teaspoon of lemon juice 
- 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder 
- Salt and pepper, to taste 

Instructions: 
1. In a large bowl, mix together the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, celery, red onion, and parsley. 

2. Add the lemon juice, garlic powder, and salt and pepper, and mix until everything is combined. 

3. Taste and adjust the seasoning, if needed. 

4. Serve the chicken salad over a bed of lettuce, on a sandwich, or on its own.
