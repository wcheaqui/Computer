Ingredients:
- 2 tilapia fillets
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons fish sauce
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Place the tilapia fillets in a lightly greased baking dish.
3. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, soy sauce, honey, fish sauce, oregano, pepper, and salt.
4. Pour the mixture over the tilapia fillets.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until the fish is easily flaked with a fork.
6. Serve with brown rice and steamed kale.
