Ingredients:

1 lb. ground turkey
4 cups baby spinach or mixed greens
1/2 cup crumbled feta or goat cheese
1/2 cup diced tomatoes
1/4 cup diced onions
1/4 cup sliced black olives
1/4 cup homemade salsa (see recipe below)
1/4 cup low-fat or fat-free sour cream
Cooking spray
Salt and pepper, to taste
For the salsa:

3 medium-sized tomatoes, diced
1/2 small onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
Juice of 1 lime
1/4 tsp. ground cumin
Salt and pepper, to taste
Optional: diced bell peppers, diced cucumber, or chopped parsley as a cilantro substitute
Instructions:

1. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and spray with cooking spray.
2. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the diced tomatoes, onion, and garlic for the salsa. Add the lime juice, ground cumin, and optional ingredients if desired. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Add the ground turkey to the skillet and cook until browned, about 8-10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. While the turkey is cooking, prepare the salad ingredients. In a large bowl, mix together the baby spinach or mixed greens, crumbled feta or goat cheese, diced tomatoes, diced onions, and sliced black olives.
5. Once the turkey is cooked, add it to the salad bowl.
6. Drizzle the homemade salsa and low-fat or fat-free sour cream on top of the salad.
7. Toss the salad gently to mix all the ingredients together.
Serve immediately.
