Ingredients:
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized cubes
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, grated
- 2 cups cooked white rice
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Chopped green onion and sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil, onion, bell pepper, garlic and ginger. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until vegetables are soft and fragrant.

2. Add the chicken cubes and cook for about 8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through.

3. Add the cooked white rice, soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, and rice vinegar. Stir to combine and let cook for another 5 minutes.
