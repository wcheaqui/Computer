
Ingredients
• 2 large zucchini, sliced into 1/4-inch thick rounds
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
• Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions
1. Preheat air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine zucchini, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Toss until zucchini is evenly coated.
3. Place zucchini in the air fryer basket in a single layer.
4. Cook for 8-10 minutes, or until zucchini is golden brown and tender.
5. Serve warm. Enjoy!
