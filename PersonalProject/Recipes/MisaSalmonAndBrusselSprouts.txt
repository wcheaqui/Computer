Sure, here's a recipe for Miso-Glazed Salmon with Brussels Sprouts: 

Ingredients: 
- 4 (6-ounce) salmon fillets
- 2 tablespoons white miso paste
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
- 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 cups Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved 

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.

2. In a small bowl, mix together miso paste, honey, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and pepper.

3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Once heated, add the salmon fillets and cook for about 2 minutes per side, or until golden brown.

4. Pour the miso mixture over the salmon and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.

5. Add the Brussels sprouts to the skillet and stir to combine.

6. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 10 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the Brussels sprouts are tender.

7. Serve the Miso-Glazed Salmon with Brussels Sprouts warm. Enjoy!
